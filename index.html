Part I — Always Free

Your allergy log, as it should be.

Log Symptoms in Seconds

Tap to record cough, congestion, runny nose, and dozens more. Every entry saves to Apple Health — yours to keep, search, and share with your doctor.

Patterns, Not Guesses

See your weekly trends, most frequent symptoms, and which weather conditions tend to make you feel worse. All from data you already logged.

Tomorrow, Predicted

After two weeks of logging, PollenPath predicts tomorrow's symptom severity from the weather forecast — entirely on your device.

Yours Alone

Symptoms live in Apple Health on your device. Location is city-level only. No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no data sold — ever.

Everything above is free. No account, no trial, no credit card. Part II covers what Pro layers on top.

Pollen and air quality coverage varies by region. PollenPath tells you what's available for your area during onboarding.
