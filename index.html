Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is PollenPath a medical app?
No. PollenPath is a wellness and tracking tool — it helps you notice patterns between how you feel and what's in the air. It does not provide medical advice or diagnosis. For medical concerns, talk to a clinician.
Where does the data come from?
Environmental data is provided by the Google Pollen API, Google Air Quality API, and Apple WeatherKit. Species coverage varies by region and may not include every locally relevant allergen.
How is my privacy protected?
Your symptom logs and the machine learning that finds your triggers stay on your device. There are no accounts, no third-party trackers, and no ads. You're in control of what syncs to Apple Health.
What do I get for free?
Symptom logging, live weather, Apple Health integration, and your Top Symptoms insight are free forever. Basic and Pro add today's pollen index, air quality, longer history, forecasts, and on-device predictions.
Which devices are supported?
PollenPath is a native iOS app for iPhone, with Home-Screen widgets. Download it free from the App Store and start logging in seconds.
