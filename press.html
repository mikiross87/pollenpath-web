About the app

Short (one line)

PollenPath: Allergy Tracker. Pollen forecast and air quality, with on-device AI insights that connect your symptoms to what’s in the air. Always free, with optional Basic and Pro tiers.

Medium (~100 words)

Track allergy symptoms alongside real-time pollen forecasts and air quality data. Discover your personal triggers with on-device AI insights.

PollenPath connects your symptoms to real-time pollen, air quality, and weather data — then uses on-device AI to reveal your personal triggers. No guessing, no tracking, no ads. Symptom logging is always free and syncs to Apple Health; live pollen and air quality unlock with Basic, and Pro adds the 5-day forecast plus species-level detail.

Long (~250 words)

Finally understand your allergies. PollenPath connects your symptoms to real-time pollen, air quality, and weather data — then uses on-device AI to reveal your personal triggers. No guessing, no tracking, no ads.

Know what’s in the air. Check real-time pollen levels and air quality for your area. See which trees, grasses, and weeds are active right now — and what’s coming in the next 5 days.

Log symptoms in seconds. Quickly record how you’re feeling with a tap-based logger. Cough, congestion, runny nose, and dozens more — all stored securely in Apple Health.

Discover your triggers. PollenPath analyzes your symptom patterns alongside pollen and weather data — all on your device — to reveal personal correlations.

Privacy first. Your health data never leaves your device. Pollen lookups use city-level location only — never your exact coordinates. No analytics, no tracking, no ads.

Three plans: Free (symptom logging, weather, Apple Health), Basic (live pollen and air quality), Pro (5-day forecast, species-level detail, personalized insights).
